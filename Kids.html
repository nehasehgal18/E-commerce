Objective
The Kids’ Department serves individuals who are chronologically and developmentally ages 0-11 and their families. We strive to provide a welcoming space where families can learn and spend time together. We provide regular programming for all kids of all ages as well as special programming throughout the year. For up-to-date information on programs currently being offered, or to sign-up for a program please visit the library’s web calendar.
